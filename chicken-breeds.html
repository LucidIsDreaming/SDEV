Popular Chicken Breeds

Leghorn

Leghorns are active, hardy, and excellent layers of white eggs. They are often chosen for commercial egg production.

  • Origin: Italy
  • Egg Color: White
  • Temperament: Energetic, flighty

Rhode Island Red

Rhode Island Reds are valued for both their brown eggs and meat. They are hardy and adaptable to various climates.

  • Origin: United States
  • Egg Color: Brown
  • Temperament: Docile, hardy

Silkie

Silkies are ornamental chickens with soft, fur-like feathers. They are gentle and often kept as pets.

  • Origin: China
  • Egg Color: Cream
  • Temperament: Friendly, calm

Chicken Lifestyles

  1. Free-Range: Chickens roam outdoors, foraging for insects and plants. This lifestyle promotes natural behaviors and healthier birds.
  2. Backyard: Chickens are kept in small flocks in home gardens. They provide eggs and pest control, and are often treated as pets.
  3. Commercial: Large flocks are raised for egg or meat production, often indoors with controlled environments.
  4. Show/Ornamental: Some breeds are kept for exhibition or as ornamental pets due to their unique appearance.
