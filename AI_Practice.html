Welcome to the ultimate resource for chicken enthusiasts! Here, you will find everything you need to know about chickens, from breeds and care to health and nutrition.

Fun Chicken Facts

  • Chickens can remember over 100 different faces of people or animals.
  • They communicate with more than 30 types of vocalizations.
  • Chickens are the closest living relatives to the Tyrannosaurus rex!
  • Mother hens talk to their chicks while they are still in the egg.
